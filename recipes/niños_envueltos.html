Niños Envueltos

Description

"Niños Envueltos" are a delicacy from Arabia. They are made from meat and rice, and they are wrapped by a leaf which can be from many vegetables, but the original recipe is with leaves from the vineyard.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Rice
  • Leaves
  • Spice

Steps

  1. First mix the rice, meat and spice.
  2. Then cook the leaves.
  3. Wrap the rice and meat with the leaves.
  4. Finish cooking everything!
