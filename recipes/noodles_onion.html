Onion Pasta

Description

"Onion Pasta" is a simple, tasty dish we cook at home. It is basically any pasta you want, we choose spaghettis mostly, and then make a caramelized onion sauce, with or without some cream.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onions
  • Sugar
  • Spice

Steps

  1. First boil the water.
  2. Then chop the onions and cook them with lots of sugar.
  3. Cook the pasta as you finish the onions.
  4. Serve the caramelized onions with the pasta.
